# O'zGezer Brand Foundation

## Identity

- Product name: `O'zGezer`
- Primary domain target: `ozgezer.uz`
- Secondary domain fallback: `ozgezer.com`
- Product category: Uzbekistan travel discovery platform

## Positioning

O'zGezer helps two audiences at the same time:

- tourists who want to discover the country's iconic and lesser-known destinations
- local residents who want trusted recommendations before visiting another region

The core promise is simple: users should be able to answer "Is it worth going there?" through real reviews, practical details, and place discovery tools.

## Brand Direction

- Tone: warm, practical, trustworthy, exploratory
- Personality: modern Uzbek travel guide, locally grounded and visually confident
- Visual goals: clean information hierarchy, scenic atmosphere, premium but accessible feel

## Color System

Use these as the initial design tokens for Phase 1 UI work.

| Token | Hex | Usage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `--color-sky` | `#1B6CA8` | primary actions, links, key accents |
| `--color-teal` | `#167C74` | map and exploration accents |
| `--color-gold` | `#D4A63D` | highlights, badges, editorial moments |
| `--color-sand` | `#F4E7C8` | soft surfaces and cards |
| `--color-ink` | `#0F172A` | headings and dark text |
| `--color-mist` | `#ECF4F3` | subtle backgrounds and panels |

## Typography Direction

- Headings: classy serif display with strong destination feel
- Body: clean sans-serif with good Cyrillic and Latin support
- Recommended pairings for design exploration:
  - `Cormorant Garamond` + `Manrope`
  - `Lora` + `IBM Plex Sans`

## Logo Direction

- Wordmark-first logo for fast launch
- Potential icon motifs: compass, route line, arch silhouette, sun over horizon
- Avoid cluttered tourism clichés and over-ornamented marks

## Product Principles

- Prioritize clarity over decoration
- Keep local trust signals visible
- Make map, reviews, and region context feel first-class
- Support Uzbek, Russian, and English from the beginning
